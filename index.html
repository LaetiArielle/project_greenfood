GREEN FOOD

we care about your health

Green food is a vegetarian and organic restaurant and our daily objective is to provide well-being, to create, serve and taste organic, local and refined products. We love fresh, tasty, seasonal and original food! Come discover the meaning of these words at our place during a lunch or dinner. Here we propose a viaraty of healthy meals with their ingredients and recipe which can enable you to reproduce them on your own.

Discover our Restaurant story

Green Food was born from my deep love for fruits and vegetables, cereals, legumes for healthy and authentic vegetarian cuisine. A handful of herbs, a pinch of spices, a few inspirations from around the world, tasty and local seasonal products, all sprinkled with joy and colors products. Graduated from "Ecole Supérieure de Cuisine Française" in Paris, I worked as a chef in France and abroad. A few years of culinary styling in Paris enriched my know-how and led me towards culinary journalism, I gradually moved towards healthy cuisine which I shared at tables d'hôte and through cooking workshops. innovative and gourmet vegetarian.
